Advanced Technical Indicators

  • Email headers show signs of spoofing
  • Missing SPF/DKIM authentication
  • Domain registered recently (14 days ago)
  • IP address location mismatch with claimed origin
  • URL redirects through multiple suspicious domains

Recommendations

  • Always verify the sender's email address before responding.
  • Don't click on suspicious links in emails.
  • Enable two-factor authentication for important accounts.
